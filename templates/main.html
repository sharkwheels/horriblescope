What is it?

A twitter bot that tweets terrible, sarcastic, and cheeky, horrorscopes at you. You can visit @horrible_scope to try it out!

How To Use It?

Simply tweet something like: "I would like a reading. I'm a Taurus" at @horrible_scope. And it will answer you. You don't have to include your sign, but you can if you want. Your tweet however, must include the word reading, or the bot will get annoyed.

Who Made It?

Made by NL. Readings contributed by a squad of cranky oracles...or some darkly humoured pals (Who might also be oracles. On the internet, no one is ever really sure).
